HTML Lists

There are three types of lists in HTML:

Unordered Lists, Ordered Lists, Definition Lists

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are also called bullet lists and have a bullet pre-fixed before every list element. They are enclosed within <ul></ul> tags. Each list element is enclosed within <li></li> tags.

Lets take an example:

Cake Ingredients

Milk Flour Sugar Baking powder Nuts Cocoa

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists are also called numbered lists and have a number pre-fixed before every list element. They are enclosed within <ul></ul> tags. Each list element is enclosed within <li></li> tags.

Lets take an example:

Subjects in Class

Mathematics Science English Hindi Social Studies

Definition Lists

Definition lists consist of lists of terms and their definitions. They are enclosed within <dl></dl> tags. Each term is enclosed within <dt></dt> tags and definition within <dd></dd> tags.
